What does iDEX stand for?

"I" for Incubator

Do you have genius ideas about app development but have trouble with finding kindred spirits? In iDEX, you will meet passionate teammates and together incubate any stunning idea you come up with!

ideas visions
"D" for Design

Design is an essential part in application development. It determines users' initial perception and subsequent user expeiences with the product. We pursue design with high effectiveness and aesthetic value.

gorgeous creative
"E" for Efficiency

Efficiency is one of the main reasons why computers exist. As programmers and designers, we embrace it as one of our principles and strive to build easy-to-use controls and libraries for various platforms.

efficient intuitive
"X" for Experience

Your experience matters. For users, iDEX denotes to enhance your UX with delibrately designed interaction logics. For our members, iDEX endeavors to enrich your experience with your major skills.

fluency simplicity
